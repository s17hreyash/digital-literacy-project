# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Practices
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for all digital accounts
- Enable two factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown attachments
- Always verify the authenticity of websites before entering sensitive information

## UPI and Financial Safety
- Remember: Entering a UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it
- Do not accept payment requests from unknown individuals
- Avoid scanning QR codes received from untrusted sources
- Use only official and trusted payment apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
- Immediately check transaction details before approving any payment

## Safe Online Behavior
- Do not share personal information (address, phone number, bank details) publicly
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly update apps and devices for security patches
- Log out from accounts on shared or public devices

## Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report fraud immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
- Inform your bank as soon as possible to block further transactions
