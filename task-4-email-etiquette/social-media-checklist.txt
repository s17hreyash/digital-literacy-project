# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and clean profile
- Think carefully before posting any content
- Use privacy settings to control visibility
- Verify information before sharing it online
- Respect others and communicate responsibly

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
- Avoid engaging in cyberbullying or negative behavior
- Do not spread fake or unverified information
- Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language
- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
